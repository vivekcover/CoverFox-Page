Renew your Lapsed Car Insurance Policy Today!

Getting Started

A motor insurance policy considered to be a case of ‘break in’ or considered lapsed if the last day of renewal has been missed. Your car insurance is mandatory hence there is serious consequence if your policy gets lapsed.

Not only are you legally liable, even the insurance company will need to get your vehicle inspected by a surveyor who will then ascertain if there are any damages to the car, and would ascertain if the car is eligible for the insurance or not. This makes the process of renewal longer, more expensive and may be tedious. There is also a high chance you may not get a suitable quote which you may have been otherwise entitled to, had your policy not lapsed.

If you miss the deadline by 90 days then you will lose your no claim bonus which in some cases can be upto 50% discount on your premium. This is a substantial saving that you stand to lose.
